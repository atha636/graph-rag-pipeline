Tesla was founded in 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning.

Elon Musk joined Tesla as chairman and lead investor in 2004.

Elon Musk became CEO of Tesla in 2008.

Tesla developed the Model S, which became the best-selling electric car in 2015 and 2016.

Tesla developed the Model 3, designed to be an affordable mass-market electric vehicle.

Tesla operates Gigafactories to manufacture batteries and electric vehicles at scale.

Tesla developed Autopilot, an advanced driver assistance system using cameras and neural networks.

Tesla energy division produces Powerwall, a home battery storage product.

Tesla acquired SolarCity in 2016 to expand into solar energy.

Tesla became the most valuable car company in the world by market cap in 2020.
